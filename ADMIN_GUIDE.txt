================================================================================
INNOVIX LLC - ADMIN DASHBOARD MANAGEMENT GUIDE
================================================================================

This guide provides the necessary steps to access and manage the admin dashboard 
features for the InnovixLLC platform.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. ACCESSING THE DASHBOARD
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- URL: /admin
- Requirement: You must be logged in with an account that has the "ADMIN" role.
- If you are not an admin, you will be redirected to the login page or home.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. PRODUCT INVENTORY MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Location: /admin/inventory

A. Creating a New Product:
   1. Click the "Add New Product" button or go to /admin/inventory/new.
   2. Fill in basic info (Title, Category, SKU, Description).
   3. Set the Selling Price and MSRP.
   4. Add Features (Trust signals) and System Requirements.
   5. Upload a product image (Max 5MB) or paste an image URL.
   6. Enter initial license keys (one per line) in the "Inventory Keys" section.
   7. Click "Publish Product" to make it live.

B. Editing a Product:
   1. In the Inventory list, click the "Edit" icon (pencil) next to a product.
   2. Update any fields and click "Save Changes".

C. Deleting a Product:
   1. Click the "Trash" icon next to the product.
   2. Confirm the deletion.
   *Note: This will also delete associated unsold keys and detach order history. if you dont want to delete the product just set status to draft. 
   The products which are in draft will not be visible in the website.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. DIGITAL KEY (STOCK) MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Location: /admin/inventory

A. Adding New Keys to Existing Products:
   1. In the Inventory list, find your product and click "Manage Keys" (or the Plus icon).
   2. In the modal, paste your new keys (one per line).
   3. Click "Confirm Upload".

B. Clearing Unsold Stock:
   1. Click "Manage Keys" on a product.
   2. In the "Danger Zone" section, click "Clear All Unsold Stock".
   3. This is useful for rotating keys or fixing bulk upload errors.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. BLOG MANAGEMENT
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Location: /admin/blogs

A. Creating a Blog Post:
   1. Go to /admin/blogs/new.
   2. Enter Title and Content (Supports Markdown).
   3. Add Tags and "Key Takeaways" (These appear at the top of the blog).
   4. Upload a featured image.
   5. Set status to "PUBLISHED" or "DRAFT".

B. Editing a Blog Post:
   1. Click the "Edit" icon in the blog list.
   2. Update content, takeaways, or visibility and save.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
5. MONITORING & STATS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Stats: Use the top cards in the Inventory page to monitor Total Inventory, 
  Active Listings, and Low Stock Alerts (< 10 keys).
- System Logs: View the "System Log" at the bottom of the Inventory page to 
  track admin actions (Price updates, new stock, product creation).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
TECH SUPPORT NOTE:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
If you encounter "Permission Denied" or database errors:
1. Ensure your database is synced (npx prisma db push).
2. Ensure you have the correct ADMIN role assigned to your email in the database.

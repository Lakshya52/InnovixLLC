INNOVIX LLC - PLATFORM OVERVIEW & USER GUIDE
================================================================================

Welcome to the InnovixLLC Platform! This guide is designed to help you 
understand all the features and capabilities of your new website, explained 
 in simple terms for business owners and daily users.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1. THE VISITOR EXPERIENCE (Your Public Website)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This is the "storefront" that everyone sees. It is designed to be fast, 
beautiful, and easy to use on both computers and mobile phones.

- Professional Storefront: A clean homepage that highlights your best products 
  and latest news to build trust with new visitors.
- Product Catalog: A organized list where customers can browse software by 
  category (like "Productivity" or "Security") and find exactly what they need.
- Detailed Product Pages: Each item has its own page with clear descriptions, 
  price tags, and a list of "what you get" (features) to help customers decide.
- Secure Shopping Cart: Customers can add multiple items to their cart and 
  see exactly what they are buying before they pay.
- Learning Hub (Blog): A place for you to share articles, tips, and updates. 
  Each article includes "Key Takeaways" to help readers get the main points quickly.
- Instant Help: A built-in Chatbot and Contact Form ensure that your 
  customers can always reach you if they have questions.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
2. THE CUSTOMER DASHBOARD (For Your Clients)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
When a customer creates an account, they get their own private "Office" area 
to manage their relationship with your business.

- Order History: A clean list of everything they have ever bought from you, 
  including dates and prices.
- Digital Vault (My Keys): This is the most important part! Customers can 
  securely view and copy the software license keys they purchased at any time.
- Personal Support Center: If a customer has an issue, they can open a 
  "Support Ticket." It works like a private chat room between you and them.
- Profile Management: Customers can update their name, email, and even 
  upload a profile picture to personalize their experience.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
3. THE BUSINESS COMMAND CENTER (For You / Admin)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
This is your private dashboard (located at /admin) where you run your business.

- Business Health Snapshot: A quick-look dashboard showing your total sales, 
  number of customers, and how many support requests need your attention.
- Inventory Control: 
  - Easily add new software products with images and descriptions.
  - Bulk Upload: You can add hundreds of license keys at once just by pasting them.
  - Low Stock Alerts: The system automatically warns you when you are running 
    out of keys for a specific product.
- Customer Management: 
  - View a list of all your customers and see when they were last active.
  - Security Controls: You have the power to block suspicious accounts or 
    remove users if necessary.
- Order Tracking: A master list of every transaction on the platform, so 
  you can keep your books in order.
- Content Creator: A simple tool to write and publish blog posts without 
  needing to know any "coding."
- Support Desk: Respond to customer questions in real-time and mark them as 
  "Resolved" once the customer is happy.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
4. PEACE OF MIND FEATURES
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Automated Emails: The system sends automatic "Thank You" emails and 
  Account Verification codes so you don't have to do it manually.
- Security First: All passwords are encrypted, and your database is protected 
  to keep your business and customer data safe.
- Mobile Ready: Everything—from the shop to the admin dashboard—works 
  perfectly on tablets and smartphones.
